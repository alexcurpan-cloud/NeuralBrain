## Daily Rule (starting Monday, June 15, 2026)

Every day, regardless of mood or energy:

**1. ONE PUBLIC POST / UPDATE**
- Argus writes the text, Alex posts on Moltbook + X

- Alex writes code, Argus writes the commit message
